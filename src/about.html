About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2011, Loop Studios began as a small team of VR enthusiasts with a big vision. What started in a garage with basic equipment has evolved into one of the world's leading virtual reality studios.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation and a relentless pursuit of immersive excellence. We've pioneered new techniques in spatial audio, haptic feedback, and visual rendering that have set industry standards.

"We don't just create virtual realities—we craft meaningful experiences that change how people see the world."
— Maria Chen, Founder & CEO
