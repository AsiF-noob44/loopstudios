Frequently Asked Questions

To set up your LoopVR headset:

  1. Charge your headset for at least 2 hours before first use
  2. Download the LoopVR companion app on your smartphone
  3. Follow the in-app setup instructions to connect your headset
  4. Create or sign in to your LoopVR account
  5. Complete the guided setup process in the headset

Software updates are automatically downloaded when your device is connected to Wi-Fi and has sufficient battery (above 50%). To manually check for updates:

  1. Go to Settings in your headset menu
  2. Select System > Software Update
  3. If an update is available, follow the on-screen instructions

All LoopVR hardware products come with a standard 1-year limited warranty that covers manufacturing defects. Premium products like the LoopVR Pro X come with an extended 2-year warranty. For software products, we offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee.

To check your warranty status or make a claim, please visit our warranty portal or contact customer support.
